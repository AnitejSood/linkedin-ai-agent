### 3.2 LLM-Powered Topic Selection (Major Improvement)

**Old Method:**

**New Method:**  
A **three-stage LLM pipeline**:
1. **Scoring (70 articles)** ‚Äî Gemini ranks each 1‚Äì10  
2. **Top 10 Pre-filtering** ‚Äî Removes duplicates & old topics  
3. **Final Selection** ‚Äî Gemini picks 1 optimal for engagement  

**Scoring Factors (Weights):**
- Business Impact ‚Äì 25%  
- Discussion Potential ‚Äì 25%  
- Viral Potential ‚Äì 20%  
- Timeliness ‚Äì 15%  
- Professional Value ‚Äì 15%  

üí∞ **Cost:** ~$0.045 per selection  
‚ö° **Efficiency:** 85% cheaper vs na√Øve LLM calls
